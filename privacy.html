Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Seeny ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using Seeny, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register, we collect your email address, full name, and username to create and manage your account securely.

2.2 User-Generated Content

We collect and store the photos, messages, reactions, and "Vibes" (emojis) that you post or share within the app to enable social interactions between you and your friends.

2.3 Precise Location Data

With your permission, we collect precise location data (GPS coordinates) to provide core features such as:

  • Displaying the place name (District/City) where a photo was captured.
  • Providing real-time weather conditions and temperature at your location.
  • Calculating and displaying altitude data for your posts.

2.4 Social Data

We manage your "Social Graph," which includes your list of friends, pending friend requests, and blocked users, to ensure a safe and personalized social experience.

2.5 Device Permissions

Seeny requires access to your device's Camera, Photo Library, and Location Services to function as intended.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To authenticate users, enable photo sharing, deliver messages, and calculate streaks.
  • Product Personalization: To customize your timeline, memories, and widgets based on your social circle.
  • Analytics: To evaluate user behavior and improve app performance through the effectiveness of features like push notifications.

4. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to help operate our service:

  • Supabase: For secure database storage, file hosting (Storage), and user authentication. Your data is encrypted during transit and at rest.
  • OneSignal: To deliver push notifications (e.g., friend requests, new messages). OneSignal may collect device identifiers to ensure reliable delivery.
  • Apple WeatherKit: To provide accurate weather information based on your precise location.

5. User Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Seeny provides a "Delete Account" option in the app settings. Upon initiating account deletion, all your personal data, photos, and social connections will be permanently removed from our active databases in compliance with Apple's requirements.
