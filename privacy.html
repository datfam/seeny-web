Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide the Seeny experience, we collect:

  • Account Data: Email, Full Name, and Username.
  • User Content: Photos (Polaroids), messages, and emotional "Vibes" you share.
  • Device Permissions: Camera and Photo Library access for capturing and posting moments.

2. Data Storage & Security

We use Supabase as our primary infrastructure provider to ensure your data is stored securely and encrypted. Your personal information is only accessible to you and the friends you choose to connect with.

3. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can edit your profile or delete specific posts at any time. Seeny also provides a "Delete Account" feature within the app settings, which will permanently remove all your data from our servers.
