Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Seeny ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register, we collect your email address, full name, and username to create and manage your account.

2.2 User-Generated Content

We collect and store the photos (Polaroids), messages, reactions, and "Vibes" that you post or share within the app.

2.3 Device Permissions

Seeny requires access to your device's Camera and Photo Library to allow you to capture and share moments.

3. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

We utilize Supabase (an infrastructure-as-a-service provider) for secure data storage and user authentication. Your data is stored on secure servers and is encrypted during transit and at rest.

4. User Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. Seeny provides a "Delete Account" option in the app settings. Upon initiating account deletion, all your personal data and content will be permanently removed from our active databases.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: datfam@seeny.app
